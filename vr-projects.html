Virtual Faraday Wing, University VR Open-Day

This is a Virtual Reality University Open Day project made for London Southbank University where the player can explore the premises and even join a multiplayer environment where they can socialize with other future students.

Doge VR

Doge VR is a VR dog simulator with gameplay elements where the player is embodied in a family dog's body, allowing the player to experience how it feels to be a dog. The experience includes quests, interactions, unique movement implementation and visual colour changes to simulate how a dog sees the world.

Life of a Shut-In

This is a quick public awareness VR experience where the player is embodied as a shut-in person. The goal of this experience is to show how shut-ins live and expose their everyday struggles. Hopefully, it will change the way they are perceived by society.

RhythmLess

RhythmLess is a rhythm game where the player uses the arrow keys to click to the beat. The game was made in Unity3D and it was designed by an iteration process where I started off with a very bare-bones project drawn on paper and finished with the project you see now, all the assets were also hand-drawn by me.
